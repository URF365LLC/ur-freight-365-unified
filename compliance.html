Carrier standards that protect every load.

UR Freight 365 LLC treats carrier compliance as an operating discipline. Carrier relationships are reviewed for authority, safety, insurance, identity, and fraud risk before freight is tendered.

Strict carrier vetting

Authority verification, safety score checks, active operating status, and insurance verification are reviewed before activation.

FMCSA standards

Carriers are expected to meet applicable FMCSA compliance expectations, including safety fitness, authority, and financial responsibility requirements.

Fraud screening

The team watches for double-brokering, stolen MC numbers, cargo theft fraud, and identity mismatches before moving freight.

Documentation discipline

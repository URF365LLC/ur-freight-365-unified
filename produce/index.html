FAQ

Common produce reefer questions.

What details should a reefer request include?

Commodity, pallet count, weight, origin, destination, pickup window, delivery appointment, set point, and receiver hours.

Can multi-stop produce loads be reviewed?

Yes. Include stop order, appointment windows, handling notes, and any cross-dock or market delivery instructions.

How are temperature notes handled?

Share set point, pre-cool expectations, pulp-check notes, seal instructions, and special handling needs in the quote request.

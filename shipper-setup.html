Broker-Shipper Packet

Set up your shipper account before the first tender.

UR Freight 365 LLC operates as a freight agent under Booking Logistics LLC | MC-1503468, an FMCSA-licensed broker. The setup process confirms your company, billing, credit, and operating terms so freight can move under the correct broker relationship.

Start Shipper SetupCall 346-522-2772

1. Complete the packet

Submit legal company name, physical and billing addresses, primary operations contacts, accounts payable contacts, lane needs, and any contract details needed for the broker-shipper agreement.

2. Provide credit references

URF365 can accept trade or freight-related credit references, billing contact details, and any requested documentation that helps Booking Logistics LLC review account terms.

3. Establish payment terms

Net 30 is the standard target once credit is approved. Different terms can be discussed based on shipment volume, lane profile, credit review, and negotiated account needs.

4. Get activated

After approval, your team can tender loads to UR Freight 365 for quoting, carrier sourcing, dispatch coordination, shipment updates, and delivery follow-through.
